For the first two summers she interned at Boston Scientific in rotation and for her last summer, she scouted a computer science professor at a local University to aid in a research project of medical record systems.

Professional Work

During the course of her academic career, she managed to accrue nearly 3 years of work experience. With the privilege of working for Centre College in a digital archivist assistant role, she learned valuable professional skills such as digital data management, programming and software systems. She worked webscarping, creating metadata, creating content and tracking statistics for social media accounts.

Software Development

During Junior year, she was granted the opportunity to work on a bioinformatics project with a Centre College computer science professor developing a customizable heat map and a program to run protein compounds through AutoCad Vina software.

In her senior year, she completed her school credit through an remote data science internship at Kami, a parenting application software company located in London, expanding the database content for their AI live chat.

Selam most appreciates her computer science education as it can be used in any field.

Her future endeavors include working with a tech team pursuing efforts in climate change, education and social equality.

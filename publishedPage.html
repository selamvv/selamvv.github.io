Creating a Graphical Tool for Non-Programmers to Use to Make Heatmaps

A heatmaps can be used to visualize or represent raw data in an easily digestible format that can enable users to understand their data.

During Junior year, I was granted the opportunity to work on a bioinformatics project with a Centre College computer science professor developing a customizable heat map and a program to run protein compounds through AutoCad Vina software. Tkinter, a module in the Python standard library which serves as a GUI toolkit.

Oral Histories

The summer of 2020, I was able to hold an internship at Centre College conducting oral history interviews for the schools diversity collection created in part with grant funds from the Associated Colleges of the South. The grant project is titled “Pathways to Diversity” and seeks to curate an archival collection that documents our institution’s process of racial desegregation, and make those materials—along with pedagogical resources—available on a digital site.
